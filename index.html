Hyderabad, IND

I born and brought up in small town called Galiveedu in Andhara pradesh, India, I started my carrier in Hyderabad and buit my extended family, one of the best city I've lived in my life

If there is one word that can be used to describe the culture in Hyderabad, it is diverse. Owing much to its very rich history, from its inception by the Qutub Shahi dynasty to the occupation of the Mughal Empire and its decline and fall, and the subsequent rise under the Asaf Jahi dynasty, the city has been the place where the traditions–both cultural and linguistic–of North India and South India meet. Hyderabad culture can also be seen through the various monuments and iconic architectural structures in the city that are testaments to the city’s glorious history, such as the Charminar and the Golconda fort. The residents of the city refer to themselves as Hyderabadis and they have developed for themselves a culture that is distinctly a mixture of both Muslim and Hindu traditions

If you ask anybody who has visited Amsterdam how they best describe it, you would get a different answer every single time.

Chicago, IL

Some of the many things Chicago is famous for are: Chicago-style hot dogs, Chicago-style (deep dish) pizza, Maxwell Street Polish Sausage, jazz music, and 1920s gangsters like Al Capone. Chicago is also known for interesting architecture like the Sears Tower, many museums, and many loyal sports fans..
